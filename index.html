Analysis of Global Layoff Trends

View Project

Analyzed global layoffs from 2020 to 2023, focusing on the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic on workforce reductions across various industries, countries, and company stages. Utilizing SQL for data cleaning and analysis, and Python for visualization, the analysis identifies significant trends such as the high impact of layoffs in well-funded companies, the pronounced effects on consumer and retail sectors, and regional disparities with the United States leading in layoffs. The analysis also reveals clustered patterns of layoffs, offering deeper insights into job market dynamics and the timing of workforce reductions.

Technologies used: SQL (MySQL), Python
